Ishan Kishan

Early Life and Career

Ishan Kishan, born on July 18, 1998, in Patna, Bihar, India, is a talented left-handed wicket-keeper batsman known for his aggressive batting style. He made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2021 and has been a promising young talent in Indian cricket.

Indian Premier League (IPL)

Ishan Kishan gained recognition through his performances in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Representing the Mumbai Indians (MI), he has been a consistent performer in the tournament and has played several match-winning innings for the team.

International Debut

Ishan Kishan earned his maiden call-up to the Indian team in 2021 during the T20 series against England. He made an impressive debut, scoring a half-century, and showcased his aggressive yet composed batting style.

Playing Style

Ishan Kishan is known for his attacking approach to batting and is particularly effective against fast bowling. He has a wide range of shots and has the ability to clear the boundaries with ease.

Personal Life

Ishan Kishan keeps his personal life private and focuses on his cricketing career. He has been a role model for many aspiring cricketers and is admired for his dedication to the game.
